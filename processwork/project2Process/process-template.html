Project 2: Poster Process

Visual Inspo

After going on Pinterest and Behance, I found poster examples for events as a way to organize my information. I also referenced art for the event like the cupsleeve designs me and the other co-vendors made. I noticed the images used either serif or curvy sans-serif typefaces. I then got my sketchbook and combined compositions I liked from the designs.

Rough Figma Iterations

I then moved to Figma and started playing around with my sketch compositions using at least two typefaces. After asking for advice from peers, I moved to colouring the roughs. It was suggested I should take pictures from the movie and so I tried limiting it to at least three combinations.

Coding Production

After referencing my figma pages, I wanted to add more imagery and ended up searching online how to create more shapes like stars, lines, ect. From previous experience I also linked fonts to make my text look more visually pleasing.
