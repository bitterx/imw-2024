A Used Notebook

A website is like a notebook. It can store your best or worst works. There can be single or grided lines defining what to place but it can always be broken. You can flip the notebook however you please, it is made for your own needs. The only restriction is the ideas you do not put onto the malleable and glaring bright paper. It is transformative. Recorded recipes, an archive of written ideas, and a sketchbook of daily doodles, you can paste and type whatever comes to mind. The dry messy ink can be spelling errors or slang only you would understand. Crumpling a piece of paper would be a simple right and left click. Maybe after a few years, you will pick up the book from a dusty forgotten file and flip through it. Laughing and feeling nostalgic over all the small ideas you came up with. The written typeface enacts a different voice you once had but have moved past. The style of drawing you diligently kept consistent needed to be updated. In the end, these aspects are a part of your being and as the notebook is being put away, you can't help but be reminded of what your thinking was back then and are glad you came so far from it.
